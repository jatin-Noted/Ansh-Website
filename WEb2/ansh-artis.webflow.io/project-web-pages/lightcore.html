Lightcore

Light core is an immersive and thrilling horror-survival video game set in a sprawling biological research lab. The game's narrative revolves around a fateful experimental gone wrong, Leading to the transformation of a once-docile centipede , Species into a menacing monster. As the player character, you assume the role of Dmitri, a skilled mechanic who unwittingly finds himself in the midst of this chaos.

Level Design

The level design process for Lightcore involves creating intricate and engaging environments that enhance the gameplay experience, support the narrative, and evoke a sense of immersion and atmosphere. The goal is to design levels that challenge players, offer a sense of exploration, and provide opportunities for both problem- solving and combat encounters.I contributed to the conceptualization and creation of various maps for the game, as well as played a significant role in designing the levels from initial ideation to their final form. Additionally, I actively participated in developing the BSP (Binary Space Partitioning) for the game maps, ensuring their structural integrity and optimizing the gameplay experience.

Asset Creation

The process of texturing and shading has been a central focus, as I meticulously crafted textures from scratch, meticulously tailored to achieve optimal results within the UNITY High Definition Render Pipeline (HDRP) settings.
